Data Protection
Personal data collected from you
Your name, contact details (mobile and or home telephone number) and email address. Your login password is only known to you and is stored encripted.
How we use the personal data
All personal data is available to view by all members logged in on the players pages. The personal contact data is maintained by you in your settings page but can also be updated by an administrator. The personal data is stored on a cloud database service which is protected by password. The personal data is to be used for communication by members to members for the purposes of arranging games.
